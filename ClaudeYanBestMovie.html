The Dark Knight is my favorite movie because it offers multiple perspectives of how one can become evil or stay "good." I also like it because it's an action packed filmed with unpredictable plot twists, and Heath Ledger's amazing acting as the Joker helps too.

As you can probably infer from the sentence above, my favorite actor in the movie is Heath Ledger. I feel like he does such an excellent job of creating a psychopathic yet clever man to fight neck to neck with Batman himself. As I was watching the Joker interrogation scene, the way he laughed maniacally and acted like someone who belonged in an insane asylum just felt so real to me.
