Lunchroom Rumble 2.0:

This game is basically where you run around like a monkey shooting doughnuts at the NPC people. The NPC are basically just sprites moving to random locations constantly and shooting in random intervals. The sprites normally start shooting slowly, and if you don't nuke the hell out of them at first they start shooting 5 bullets at a time. You have to beat a total of 3 opponents, each having more health and shorter time between shots than the previous.
